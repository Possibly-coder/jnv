Privacy Policy

This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect data when you use our app and web portal.

1. Information We Collect

  • Authentication details such as phone number or account identifier used for sign-in.
  • Basic profile and student-link data needed to show academic records.
  • Academic data such as marks, announcements, and event records provided by school staff.
  • Device token used for push notifications (for example, new score or announcement alerts).
  • Technical logs needed for security monitoring and troubleshooting.

2. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate users and provide role-based access.
  • To display student performance and school updates.
  • To send notifications for announcements, events, and score updates.
  • To improve reliability, security, and platform performance.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Information is processed only for app functionality and may be handled by trusted service providers required to operate the platform (such as authentication, notification, hosting, and database services).

4. Security

We use access controls, authentication checks, and operational safeguards to protect data. While we use reasonable measures, no system can guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for school operations, legal compliance, and service security. Data may be removed or anonymized when no longer required.

6. Your Choices

  • You can request profile updates or account-related support through your school administrator.
  • You can disable push notifications in device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

This service is intended for authorized school staff and parents/guardians. Student data is managed by the institution in line with applicable educational and data protection requirements.
